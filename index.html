What is Annotex?

Annotex is a web-based annotation platform that allows users to highlight text and attach notes directly to the content they are reading. This helps retain context and improves understanding.

Why Use Annotex?

  • Highlight important content instantly
  • Attach notes directly to selected text
  • Preserve context for better revision
  • Access annotations even after refreshing
  • Designed specially for students and readers

How Does It Work?

  1. Paste or write your content in the editor
  2. Select any portion of the text
  3. Highlight it using annotation tools
  4. Add notes linked to that highlighted text
  5. Revisit notes anytime with full context

What Makes Annotex Special?

Unlike traditional note-taking tools, Annotex keeps your notes anchored to the exact text they refer to. This contextual connection improves recall and makes studying more effective.
